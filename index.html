✨ Iconic Moment

The "Impossible" Behind-the-Backboard Shot

During the 2014-15 ABA League season with Union Olimpija Ljubljana, Blaž chased down a teammate's air ball behind the backboard and, with the shot clock expiring, looped an impossible one-handed rainbow shot over the glass and into the basket. Olimpija went on to defeat Partizan Belgrade 87-58. The shot went viral across European basketball media — and two days later, he re-enacted it on live Slovenian television.
